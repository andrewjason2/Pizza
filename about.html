The Fermentation Advantage

Standard pizza dough uses fast-acting industrial yeast, which creates dense, heavy bread structures that can feel sitting heavy in your stomach. By fermenting our sourdough slowly for 48 hours at controlled temperatures, our natural yeast builds complex, airy cellular pockets.

The outcome? An incredibly light, bubble-rich crust structure that delivers a satisfying crunch, deep rustic flavor, and is incredibly easy to digest.
